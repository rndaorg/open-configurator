# Open Configurator — Investor Pitch

> **Transforming how the world sells configurable products.**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Open Configurator** is a modern, full-stack SaaS platform that lets businesses sell complex, customizable products online with the ease of a t-shirt checkout. We combine a **3D visual configurator**, an **AI-powered rule & pricing engine**, and a **collaborative sharing layer** into a single, deployable solution.

- **Product**: White-label configurator + commerce backend
- **Stage**: Live MVP, paying-ready (Stripe + multi-tier subscriptions integrated)
- **Raising**: Seed round to accelerate GTM and enterprise features
- **Traction goal**: 100 paying SMB customers in 12 months

---

## 2. The Problem

Selling configurable products online is **broken**:

| Pain point | Impact |
|---|---|
| Static product pages can't represent thousands of variants | **Lost sales** — customers leave to call/email |
| Custom configurators cost $50k–$500k to build | **Excludes SMBs** entirely |
| Existing tools (Tacton, Configit, Epicor CPQ) target enterprise only | **Underserved mid-market** |
| Pricing logic lives in spreadsheets and sales reps' heads | **Errors, margin leakage, slow quotes** |
| No collaboration — customers can't share configs with partners/spouses | **Abandoned carts** |

> **70% of B2B buyers** want self-service configuration. Less than **10%** of mid-market sellers offer it.

---

## 3. Our Solution

A **plug-and-play configurator platform** that any business can launch in days, not months.

### Core capabilities
- 🎨 **3D Visualization** — Real-time Three.js rendering of every configuration
- ⚙️ **Rules Engine** — Visual constraint builder; no code required
- 💰 **Dynamic Pricing** — Volume tiers, conditional discounts, server-validated
- 🤝 **Collaborative Sharing** — Unique URLs, QR codes, real-time co-editing
- ❤️ **Wishlist & Save** — Customers return to finish what they started
- 🌍 **Global-ready** — 5 languages (incl. RTL Arabic), 9 currencies
- 📊 **Admin Analytics** — Conversion funnels, A/B tests, customer insights
- 🔐 **Enterprise security** — RLS, RBAC, server-side validation, Zod schemas
